About Excellent Early Years Hub

Growing confident learners, online.

Excellent Early Years Hub is a registered online brand with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria, dedicated to providing high-quality online education for learners of all ages.

We offer personalised online lessons that help children build strong academic foundations and support adults in mastering languages and other essential skills.

Quality Education From Anywhere

About the Founder

Marcel Onyinye Gift (Odoh), founder of Excellent Early Years Hub, brings over 12 years of teaching experience and a deep passion for education.

She holds a degree in Economics and a Postgraduate Diploma in Education, combining academic expertise with practical teaching skills.

Gift leads a team of qualified tutors delivering personalised online learning for children and adults worldwide, with a strong focus on building solid foundations, boosting confidence, and helping every learner achieve real and measurable results.

Why Choose Us

Excellent Early Years Hub offers personalised, high-quality online learning for children and adults. From early years education and academic support to adult language classes, our experienced tutors deliver engaging, learner-centred lessons.

We work closely with families and adult learners worldwide to nurture confidence, growth, and lasting success. Our approach is built on consistency, structured support, and a genuine commitment to each learner’s development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs

What ages do you cater to?

We provide online education for learners of all ages. Our programs range from early years support for young children to academic tutoring for school-aged learners and language learning for adults.

How are the online sessions conducted?

All our sessions are conducted via secure online platforms like Zoom or Google Meet. We use interactive tools, multimedia resources, and engaging activities to ensure effective learning experiences.

Are your tutors qualified?

Yes, all our tutors are highly qualified professionals with relevant educational backgrounds and teaching experience. They undergo rigorous training in online teaching methodologies.

How do I enroll my child or myself?

You can enroll by clicking the "Enroll Now" button which will connect you to our WhatsApp. Our team will guide you through the registration process, assess your learning needs, and match you with the most suitable tutor.
